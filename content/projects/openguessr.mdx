## Key decisions

Coverage is treated as product logic rather than luck. The location sampler works across broad regions, then falls back to known viable locations when Street View cannot produce a usable panorama.

The mobile design gives the panorama visual priority and reveals the guess map on demand. Keyboard-reachable menus and clear round states make the same flow usable beyond pointer-first desktop play.

## Architecture

The current product is a browser client backed by runtime configuration. Supabase contracts establish the room and round state needed for multiplayer, but online play is deliberately presented as in progress rather than finished.
