## Key decisions

The balance is never a second source of truth. It is derived from a contact’s transaction stream, preventing an edited total from drifting away from the activity that created it.

Sharing is an optional projection of the local ledger. Recipients receive a read-only view and can confirm or dispute entries; revocation removes the cloud snapshot while leaving the local app untouched.

## Architecture

Room is the durable local store behind a Compose UI, with Hilt, MVVM and StateFlow keeping updates predictable. Firebase is isolated to optional sharing rather than required for core app behaviour.
