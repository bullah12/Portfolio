## Key decisions

Privacy is a data-model rule, not a settings afterthought. Climbs begin private, public visibility is explicit and anonymous projections expose only the fields a public route experience needs.

Recommendations are split into two understandable jobs: goal-gap suggestions for BMG progression, and a preference ranker for personal discovery. Route imports retain licence and attribution information instead of erasing provenance during normalisation.

## Architecture

The App Router product uses Prisma and Postgres beneath Supabase Auth and Row Level Security. Geospatial editing combines MapLibre and Terra Draw, with OpenRouteService used for bounded trail following and a safe manual fallback.
