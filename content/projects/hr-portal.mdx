## Key decisions

Scoring is deterministic and inspectable: must-have skills, useful extras, experience and eligibility contribute to a weighted result, while missing essentials cap that result. People—not an algorithm—control stage changes.

Candidates use narrow tokenised links rather than receiving internal staff accounts. Provider integrations retain local fallback modes, so unfinished connections are never hidden behind a false production claim.

## Architecture

App Router APIs validate inputs before PostgreSQL persistence through Prisma. JWT cookie authentication, role middleware and append-only auditing form the security boundary around the workflow.
